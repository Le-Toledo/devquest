# Apple Review Notes

Code Quest is an educational app for learning programming through missions, lessons and practice challenges.

## Navigation

1. Sign in with a real Supabase account or use the demo account provided manually in App Store Connect.
2. On the Hub, tap Continuar Jornada to follow the calculated recommendation.
3. Open Campanha for chapters and missions.
4. Open Academia Dev for structured lessons.
5. Open Arena de Código and Code Lab for practice.
6. Open Professor Byte for educational help. AI answers may be inaccurate and should be reviewed.
7. Open Configurações > Enviar feedback to save local beta feedback.
8. Open Perfil > Segurança > Excluir minha conta to initiate account deletion once the Edge Function is deployed.

## Purchases

No real purchases, subscriptions, or external payments are available in this version. Incomplete Premium features are hidden in production.

## Offline

Some parts of the app work offline with local progress and AI fallback. Sign-in, sync and real AI require network access.

## Before submission

Add demo account credentials manually in App Store Connect. Do not store passwords in the repository.
